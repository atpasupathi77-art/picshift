About PicShift

Your privacy-first, professional image toolkit – right in your browser.

Our Mission

PicShift was created to provide powerful, easy‑to‑use image editing tools that never compromise your privacy. We believe you shouldn’t have to upload sensitive images to a server just to resize, compress, or convert them. That’s why every tool runs entirely in your browser – your files never leave your device.

Whether you’re a developer, designer, student, or just someone who needs quick image edits, PicShift gives you 17+ professional tools for free, with no registration, no watermarks, and no hidden costs.

Privacy Guarantee

We take your privacy seriously. PicShift uses no external servers, no analytics trackers, and no cookies. Every image processing operation – compression, cropping, watermarking, format conversion – happens locally using JavaScript and HTML5 canvas. Your files are never uploaded, stored, or seen by anyone else.

You can disconnect from the internet after loading the page; all tools will still work perfectly. That’s our promise.

Technology

Built with vanilla JavaScript, HTML5 Canvas, and CSS Flex/Grid. The PDF tools use pdf.js (Mozilla) and jsPDF – both run entirely client‑side. No frameworks, no bloat, just fast, reliable performance.
