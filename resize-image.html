How to use this tool

  • 1. Upload an image – Drag & drop or click to browse.
  • 2. Adjust dimensions – Enter new width or height. The aspect ratio lock keeps proportions.
  • 3. Click "Resize Image" – The resized preview appears on the right.
  • 4. Download – Save your resized image as PNG.

✅ All processing happens in your browser – no upload, completely private.

About Resizing

Resizing changes the pixel dimensions of your image. Use it to fit social media, websites, or email attachments.

  • Lock Aspect Ratio – Maintains original proportions automatically.
  • High Quality – Uses canvas API for smooth resampling.
  • Supports all formats – JPG, PNG, WebP, GIF.

100% Private & Secure
